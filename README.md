# 🛒 Sales Superstore Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is focused on analyzing the **Superstore Sales Dataset** to uncover meaningful business insights related to sales performance, profit trends, customer segments, and regional performance.

The goal is to help businesses understand what drives profit and loss, identify top-performing categories, and improve overall decision-making using data analytics and visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-selling products and categories
* Understand customer segments and behavior
* Evaluate regional and market performance
* Detect profit loss areas and opportunities for improvement

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

The dataset includes the following key fields:

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Ship Date
* Customer Name
* Segment
* Country / Region / City
* Product Category & Sub-Category
* Sales
* Quantity
* Discount
* Profit

---

## 📈 Key Analysis

### 🔹 Sales & Profit Analysis

* Total Sales and Total Profit
* Profit Margin Analysis
* Monthly and Yearly Trends

### 🔹 Category Analysis

* Best and worst performing categories
* Sub-category wise profit distribution
* Product performance comparison

### 🔹 Customer Analysis

* Customer segmentation (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
* High-value customers identification

### 🔹 Regional Analysis

* State and city-wise performance
* Region-wise sales distribution
* Loss-making regions identification

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Certain categories generate high sales but low profit due to discount impact
* Some regions consistently underperform in terms of profit
* A small number of products contribute to majority of revenue
* Seasonal trends significantly affect sales performance
* Customer segmentation helps identify high-value customers
